Famous Cakes

Sachertorte

Sometimes Sachertorte comes with added features like buttercream or marzipan — the original Sachertorte however consists only of three parts: Chocolate cake made with dark chocolate, a filling of apricot jam and a glaze of chocolate fondant.

Schwarzwälder Kirschtorte

The people from the Black Forest in Germany have invented this marvellous cake. Chocolate sponge cake layered with cherry compote and whipped cream, generously seasoned with kirschwasser (cherry brandy).

Gâteau Opéra

Layers of almond sponge cake doused in Grand Manier, coffee flavored buttercream and chocolate ganache packed into neat rectangular slices. This scrumptous piece of decadence is fittingly crowned with real gold leaf.

Red Velvet Cake

Before Dutch processing was invented, cocoapowder would turn red through the baking process. Modern red velvet cake needs the help of red food coloring.

Tarte au Citron

Also known as lemon meringue pie. A pastrybase filled with Lemon custard, topped with meringue. If you like lemon drops you will love this cake.
